DiaBeatIt User Manual

Important: DiaBeatIt is an AI companion for tracking and analysis based on your inputs. It does not diagnose disease. Clinical diagnosis and treatment decisions must come from licensed doctors.
How the model works
  • Builds features from profile biomarkers and recent daily logs.
  • Normalizes values using saved scalers from training.
  • Generates risk score with a classifier and next-glucose with a regressor.
How each result is produced
  • Risk Score: based on profile markers, fasting glucose lags, and weekly trends.
  • Predicted Next Glucose: uses profile plus recent meal, activity, sleep, and glucose behavior.
  • Top Contributing Factors: ranks inputs by estimated pressure on risk output.
  • Personalized Recommendations: created from risk level, top factors, and weekly habits.
How inputs are evaluated
  • Profile: HbA1c, cholesterol, triglycerides, body measurements, and medical history.
  • Glucose: fasting and other readings with recency and rolling averages.
  • Lifestyle: activity minutes, sleep patterns, and meal quality trends.
How often to update
  • Glucose: daily, especially fasting every morning.
  • Meals, activity, sleep: log daily for stable weekly analysis.
  • HbA1c: after each new lab result, typically every 2 to 3 months.
  • Cholesterol/triglycerides: on each new lab panel, often every 6 to 12 months.
